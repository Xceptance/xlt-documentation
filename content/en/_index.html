Documentation Hub

This is the documentation hub for the Xceptance tools XLT, XTC, and soon Neodymium.

The SaaS platform for high-scale performance testing, extensive load test management, and advanced synthetic monitoring. XTC allows you to be ahead of performance and stability issues by using a centralized testing platform that offers fine-grained control, maximum scale, and a unique monitoring approach.

Born out of necessity, Xceptance LoadTest (XLT) has grown into a premier load and performance testing tool, featuring unrivaled capabilities and scale. XLT allows local development and execution, high scale deployment, and gives you access to all collected data. Open Source under Apache License 2.0.
